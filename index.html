NavWalkAssist(N.W.A)



What is NavWalkAssist(N.W.A)?


NavWalkAssist(N.W.A) is the white cane improved. Using the power of an Arduino and a ultrasonic sensor, we were able to develop a smart stick that warns the user of potential obstacles

Specs


UltraSonic Sensor

Pin 11 - Trig

Pin 13 - Echo

VCC - 5V

GND - GND

Speaker

Pin 8 - Red

GND - Black
